Level Assessment Quiz

If this is your first time, start with Level Assessment Quiz.
You can gauge your knowledge on cyber security.
You will be asked 18 questions in total.
Don't forget to check if you have answered all the questions!
If you haven't, you won't be able to submit your answers.
Also, after submitting, you won't be able to change your answers.
Once you pass the Level Assessment Quiz, you can move onto Port Scanner Quiz.

Port Scanner Quiz

This port scanner quiz is designed to test your knowledge on port scanning. Port scanning is basically checking to see if a port is open for communication. If it's open, then it can be taken advantage of to find vulnerabilities in the system.
